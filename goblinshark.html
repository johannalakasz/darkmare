Goblin shark

Hi! My name is Mitsukurina owstoni.

I live at least 1300 m underwater.

I eat large and small fishes, other sharks and rays, squids and crabs.

I’m afraid of nothing. You can’t stop me.

Appearance
The apex predator weighs up to 210 kilos and can grow up to 3,7 m long. This animal’s most remarkable feature is its mouth. It has the ability to detach its jaw and launch its razor-sharp teeth at any unsuspecting prey. Being a carnivore, it only eats other animals, such as fish, stingray, and other sharks. The goblin shark’s skeleton is very small compared to the rest of its body, giving it a lot of wiggly excess skin.
Relation to humans
Goblin sharks are usually caught only as a bycatch. There have been specimens, caught for display purposes, but they can’t survive long in a human-made environment. However, if someone was in a presence with a goblin shark , it could be dangerous due it’s huge size.
